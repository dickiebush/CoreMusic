How Core Music Works

You create an account with Core Music. This includes your phone number and your 10 favorite artists.

We consult our variety of sources looking for new music from the artists you gave us. We are constantly working in the background so you find out about new songs within minutes of its release

When one of your artists drops a song, we send you an SMS text with a direct link to the song. This link will open Soundcloud right on your phone, so you can listen right away
